Shraddha Patil

published October 13th, 2020

Shraddha Lalita Vikas Patil is an artist based in India, who uses a carving knife and leaves with images of people and gods to create her art.

Shraddha’s journey as an artist did not begin with her craft, she has worked in the Marathi industry for a few years as an associate art director, then moved on to work for the Star Pravah Channel after getting a call. The idea of leave carving came to the artist more of a whisper as she looked through related photos of the craft. She started out with carving of names before slowly advancing into carving out photos of gods. “I had no confidence in how much I could do,” the artist mentioned.

To create her work, the artist spoke briefly her processes. “With the help of a pencil, I gently draw the outline of the photograph on the back side of the leaf and then cut it out with a carving blade.”

The artist has many plans to take her craft further in the future. She wishes to dive into the act of paper and pencil carvings as she continues her fascinating line of work.
